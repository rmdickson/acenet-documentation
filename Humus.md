# Humus

Humus is a pair of computers managed by ACENET on behalf of the Dalhousie
research group that purchased them.  They were originally integrated with 
the Nefelibata cluster, but on the retirement of Nefelibata in summer 2025 
the two machines were relocated to [Argo](Argo.html).

The machines originally named `humus216` and `humus217` no longer know those names,
they are now `argo77` and `argo78`.  Members of the owning research group should 
interact with them in the same fashion as other contributed equipment making
up the Argo cluster.  For more on that please 
see [here](Argo.html#Job_scheduling).

INFINIBAND PERFORMANCE ON ACENET
--------------------------------
At the end of 2009, ACENET deployed a high bandwidth, low latency Infiniband
interconnect at Fundy and Placentia.  Sergiy Khan has written a report 
demonstrating some impressive performance increases now being experienced by 
users, without even needing to recompile code.  For example, a GROMACS user 
running 32 processes could see a 6x performance increase.

Introducing InfiniBand at ACENET

INFINIBAND PERFORMANCE ON ACENET
--------------------------------
At the end of 2009, ACENET deployed a high bandwidth, low latency Infiniband 
interconnect at Fundy and Placentia.  Sergiy Khan has written a report
demonstrating some impressive performance increases now being experienced by
users, without even needing to recompile code.  For example, a GROMACS user
running 32 processes could see a 6x performance increase.

Opportunistic use

All four ACENET clusters (Fundy, Glooscap, Mahone, Placentia) will be available for opportunistic use by Compute Canada account holders during the 2017 resource allocation year (April 2017-March 2018). For descriptions of the clusters, their capacity and capability, see Compute Resources.

For current status, including notifications of planned service interruptions, see Cluster Status.

To obtain login credentials for ACENET clusters, visit "Apply for a consortium account" at Compute Canada and click the ACENET button.

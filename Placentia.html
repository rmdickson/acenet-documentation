Note
Please note that using the wildcard in the name of the parallel environment for a parallel job at Placentia, like so ompi*, may result in placing your job on the Green ACENET resources that are not quipped with InfiniBand. In order to avoid this, do not use the trailing wildcard character, e.g. -pe ompi 16. This arrangement is independent of the subordinate queue, and only happens when the Green ACENET resources get manually reassigned to general productions queues.

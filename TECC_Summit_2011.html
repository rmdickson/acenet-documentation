Transportation

Saint Mary's University is approximately a 40-minute drive away from the Halifax Stanfield International Airport. The AIrporter Shuttle Service runs fairly regularly from the airport to downtown for $19.50 one-way or $36.00 return. Taxi service is available curbside in the arrivals area. A one-way trip downtown is $53.00.

Parking

For those looking to park on campus, there is a $5/day fee. If you'd like us to arrange the parking pass on your behalf, please email Florence Woolaver with your name, license plate number, and province of registration. Payment can be made at registration on the 25th.

Registration and Cost

Please register by October 18, 2011 by emailing Dianne O'Leary with your name and consortium. Please indicate in the email if you have any food sensitivities.

Cost to participants will be $30, which will cover lunch on both days. Payment can be by cash or cheque only at the morning welcome on October 25. Cheques can be made out to the Institute for Computational Astrophysics.

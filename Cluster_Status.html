Outage schedule

Jobs will not be scheduled with a run time (--time=) that extends into the beginning of a planned outage period. This is so the job will not be terminated prematurely when the system goes down.

  • Siku, 2026 May 29-June 1: Electrical work in the Henrietta Harvey building of Memorial University will require that Siku be shut down on Friday afternoon, May 29 beginning at 18h30 UTC (16h00 NDT). We anticipate return to service on Monday June 1.
  • Placentia will be powered down at the same time. Return to service time is not yet determined.
  • Argo, 2026 May 29-June 1: Due to shared infrastructure, Argo will become inaccessible when Siku is powered down. We expect jobs at Argo to continue running through the outage, although it will not be possible for users to log in at Argo to access storage or submit new jobs.

Telemac-Mascaret

"TELEMAC-MASCARET is an integrated suite of solvers for use in the field of free-surface flow."

TELEMAC-MASCARET is designed with the assumption that at least some installation directories are writable by the user, which makes it unsuitable for a single central installation. However, it depends on certain libraries which are installed centrally on Siku and Alliance clusters. Here are some notes on how to install TELEMAC in your own filespace.
